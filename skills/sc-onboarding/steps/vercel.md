# Vercel credentials (STUB — for future /sc-vercel)

## `VERCEL_TOKEN` (optional)

Personal Access Token.

**How to get one**: https://vercel.com/account/tokens → Create.

**Validator**: length ≥ 24.

## `VERCEL_TEAM_ID` (optional)

Required only if the project lives under a team rather than your personal scope. Settings → General → Team ID.

**Validator**: length ≥ 8.
